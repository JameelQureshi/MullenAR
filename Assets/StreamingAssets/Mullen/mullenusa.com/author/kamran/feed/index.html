BREA, CALIFORNIA (Sept. 10th, 2019) – Mullen Technologies announces the acquisition of a new retail location in the city of Fresno, CA.  This is Mullen’s first location outside of Southern California and is the start of a great push into the Northern California (NorCal) Market.

This well-placed Fresno dealership is the ideal Northern California starting point from which Mullen can introduce and launch its brand into the NorCal market.  “Fresno is a great location for us,” says David Michery, Mullen Founder & CEO. “This store is a perfect fit for our company and the culture we’re building for the Mullen brand.”

The Fresno location offers a full-service dealership experience with a showroom, service and parts departments.  Address is 3685 N. Clovis Ave, Fresno, CA 93727. Hours are Monday to Saturday, 8 AM – 6 PM and Sunday, 10 AM – 5 PM.

For more information, please visit: www.mullenusa.com

 

About Mullen Technologies:

Mullen Technologies is a Southern California based licensed electric vehicle manufacturer with international distribution which owns several synergistic businesses including: Mullen Auto Sales, a fast-growing series of automobile dealerships throughout California, and CarHub, a new and unique digital platform that leverages AI and offers a complete, fun to use solution for buying, selling and owning a car. In addition, Mullen is currently working with several global OEM partners to provide exciting EV options that will fit perfectly into the lives of American consumers.
