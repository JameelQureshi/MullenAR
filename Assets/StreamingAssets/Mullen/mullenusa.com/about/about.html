MULLEN, Company & Brand

Mullen Technologies is a Southern California based licensed electric vehicle manufacturer with international distribution which owns several synergistic businesses including: CarHub, a new and unique digital platform that leverages AI and offers a complete, fun to use solution for buying, selling and owning a car. In addition, Mullen is currently working with several global OEM partners to provide exciting EV options that will fit perfectly into the American consumer’s lives.

One of these strategic partners is Qiantu Motor, a wholly owned subsidiary of CH-Auto, a leading automotive design and manufacturing company in China, with whom Mullen Technologies has signed a cooperation agreement in Dec 2018 to homologate and assemble cars in the US for sales in North America.
